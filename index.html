About Me

I am a talented designer and coder based in Toronto, Canada. From a young age, I have had an interest in tinkering with technology and understanding how things work. This curiosity eventually led me to pursue multiple degrees in Design. During my studies I taught myself how to code. This led to a career where I created products that combined design and code.

Over the years, I have worked with clients in various industries, including healthcare, finance, and retail. I have designed websites, mobile apps, and even physical products. My attention to detail and ability to understand a client's needs have given me the ability to synthesize a problem into a client-focused product.
