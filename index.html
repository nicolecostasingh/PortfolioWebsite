About Me

I am a talented web designer and graphic artist located in the Greater Toronto Area. Since I was a young kid, I have always had an immense passion for both technology and the arts. I have always believed that my interest will be incorporating art into digital media.I have always enjoyed drawing and sketching, and I first became familiar with the Adobe Creative Cloud suite in high school. I began by designing little logos before taking on larger projects, including editing editorials for the school yearbook. 

In the beginning phases of my career, I am open to working both full-time jobs and freelance tasks. Applying my knowledge of design has been incredibly helpful to me in completing certain tasks, such as being precise in my work, tasks, and writing code. I have also discovered that receiving constructive criticism can help me advance as a designer and that teamwork is essential.
