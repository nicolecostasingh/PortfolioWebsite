Tim Horton's Fall Campaign

This Tim Hortons fall campaign was made in collaboration with a partner for a prior Humber College branding assignment. To coincide with Tim Hortons' seasonal marketing, we decided to make fall candles. After the scent of honey cruller are two additional scents that truly define Tim Hortons and Canada. Cinnamon buns and maple dip are two more. This project's goal was to organize the release's execution and develop marketing for an important company. This contained social media materials, such as Twitter postings, Instagram photos, TikTok videos, and a schedule outlining the responsibilities assigned to each partner.
